## Description

```
Imperium Field is the crew side of Imperium Task Manager — a work-management
system for cleaning and field-service companies built around one idea: every
job gets proven, not just marked done.

YOUR DAY, NOTHING ELSE
Open the app and see today's jobs — where, when, and what needs doing. Clock
in when you start and out when you finish; your hours track themselves.

PROOF ON EVERY TASK
Work the checklist and attach a photo to each completed item. Every photo
carries the exact time it was taken, so your manager (and their client) can
see precisely when the work was finished. Submit the job when the list is
done — approvals come back in the app.

FLAG PROBLEMS FROM THE FIELD
Missing supplies, damage, a locked door? Report it with a photo. Your manager
replies right in the app, and you'll see when it's resolved.

STAY IN SYNC
A built-in chat thread connects you with the office, and your profile shows
your weekly schedule and hours at a glance.

JOINING IS SIMPLE
Your manager sends you a single-use invite code — enter it, create your
account, and your jobs are waiting. Managers run the operation from the
Imperium web console: scheduling (including recurring jobs), checklist
templates, a client book, photo review, timesheets, and reports, all updating
live as the crew works.

Imperium Field is made for teams; you'll need an invite code or a company to
get started.
```
