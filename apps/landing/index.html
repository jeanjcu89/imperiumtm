Two apps, one live system

Everything the crew does appears on the manager's screen in real time — no refreshing, no phone calls.

Manager console · web

Plan, watch, approve

A client book, reusable checklist templates, week and month schedules with recurring jobs (weekly, biweekly, monthly), estimated durations, live progress on every job, photo review, team management with invite codes, timesheets, and reports.

Field app · iPhone

Today's work, nothing else

Crew see just their day: clock in, work the checklist, snap proof photos, report issues, chat with the office, and check their weekly schedule and hours on their profile.

The timestamp is the point

When a client asks "was it actually done?", you answer with a photo and the minute it was taken. Imperium keeps that proof attached to the job, the task, and the person — forever searchable, never lost in a text thread.
