Common questions

How do I join my company?

Ask your manager for an invite code, then choose “Have an invite code?” when signing up in the app. Codes are single-use.

Why do my photos need to include a timestamp?

Photos are proof of completed work. The time each photo was taken is shown to your manager alongside the checklist item it verifies.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Profile → Delete account. See our privacy policy for exactly what is removed.

I manage a company — where do I work from?

Managers use the web console to schedule jobs, review photo proof, reply to issues, and track hours. Sign in from the same account you created your company with.
