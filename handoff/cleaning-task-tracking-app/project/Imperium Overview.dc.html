4 mobile screens · 9 console views · one live data model
Part 01
The employee app
iOS & Android. Clock in, work the checklist, prove it with photos, and stay in touch — all from the job site.

Everything the cleaner sees first. A live clock-in timer tracks hours on the job, and the day's jobs are listed with client, address, arrival time and a progress bar for each.

Part 02
The management
console
A browser-based backend for the office. Everything the crew does on the phone flows here in real time — assign, track, review and report.
Console · 01
Dashboard
The morning command center. Active jobs, work completed today, submissions waiting for review and hours logged — over a live job board and rolling activity feed.
Console · 02
Schedule
A weekly crew board. See who's assigned where Monday through Friday, and spot open days or overloaded staff at a glance before the week starts.
Console · 03
Jobs & checklists
Every job in one table — client, location, assignee, time, live progress and status — so nothing slips between assignment and sign-off.
Console · 04
Team
The roster at a glance — who's online, how many jobs they have today and hours worked this week, per employee.
Console · 05
Photo review
The quality gate. Every submitted job's proof photos land in one grid — approve to close it out, or send back for rework.
Console · 06
Hours & timesheets
Clock-in data rolls straight into timesheets — Mon–Fri hours per employee with weekly totals, ready to hand to payroll.
Console · 07
Clients & locations
Each site with its visit frequency and the access notes and rules crews need — alarm codes, keyholders, confidentiality limits.
Console · 08
Checklist templates
Reusable task lists by service type — office, medical, food service, post-construction — so a new job inherits the right checklist and photo rules instantly. This is what makes the app work beyond cleaning.
Console · 09
Reports
The numbers that matter — on-time completion, photo compliance, average job duration and the week-over-week completion trend.
